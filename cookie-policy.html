Understanding Cookies at Everything Converter 169

This Cookie Policy explains how Everything Converter 169 uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to provide, improve, and protect our services. Everything Converter 169 uses cookies responsibly to ensure our 3,000+ unit converters across 21 categories work seamlessly across all devices and browsers. Our cookie usage is designed to improve your experience without compromising your privacy, and we provide clear controls for managing your cookie preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your device's hard drive and stored there.

Cookies do not contain any personal information that can identify you individually, but personal information we store about you may be linked to the information stored in and obtained from cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies to provide and improve our services:

Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly and cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling in forms. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.

Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit most often and if you receive error messages. This information is used to improve how our website works and to identify and fix technical issues.

Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your preferred units or language) and provide enhanced, more personalized features. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

How We Use Cookies

Everything Converter 169 uses cookies for several important purposes that enhance your experience and help us provide better services:

Service Functionality

We use cookies to ensure our conversion tools work properly across different browsers and devices. This includes remembering your preferred units, maintaining your place in multi-step conversions, and ensuring consistent functionality during your session. These cookies help us provide a smooth, uninterrupted experience while using our converters.

Functionality cookies also remember your preferences for display settings, such as theme choices or layout preferences, so you don't have to reconfigure these settings each time you visit our platform. This personalization makes our tools more convenient and user-friendly.

Performance and Analytics

We use performance cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website, which helps us improve our services. These cookies collect information about which converters are most popular, how long users spend on different pages, and which features are most valuable to our users.

This information is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to identify individual users. We use this data to optimize our platform's performance, identify and fix technical issues, and make data-driven decisions about new features and improvements.

Security and Fraud Prevention

We use cookies to help protect our platform and users from security threats and fraudulent activities. These cookies help us identify suspicious behavior, prevent automated attacks, and ensure that our services are used appropriately. Security cookies are essential for maintaining the integrity and safety of our platform.

Third-Party Cookies

We may work with trusted third-party services that set cookies on our behalf to help us provide better services. These third parties are carefully selected and must comply with strict privacy and security standards. Analytics Services

We may use third-party analytics services to help us understand how our website is used and to improve our services. These services use cookies to collect information about your use of our website, which is then used to compile reports and help us improve our website. The information collected includes the number of visitors, where visitors come from, and which pages they visit.

We use CDNs to ensure our website loads quickly for users around the world. CDNs may set cookies to optimize content delivery and improve loading times. These cookies help us provide a faster, more reliable experience for users regardless of their geographic location.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies and controlling how they are used on your device:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies, accept only certain types of cookies, or notify you when cookies are being set. However, please note that if you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly.

Each browser has different methods for managing cookies. Here are links to cookie management instructions for popular browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Internet Explorer. We recommend checking your browser's help documentation for the most current instructions.

Opt-Out Options

For cookies set by third-party services, you can often opt out directly through the third-party provider's website. Many analytics and advertising companies also provide opt-out mechanisms for their services. We provide information about these options where applicable.

You can also use tools like the Network Advertising Initiative's opt-out tool or the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out tool to manage advertising cookies from multiple companies at once.

Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different lifespans depending on their purpose:

Session Cookies: These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They are used to maintain your session while you navigate our website.

Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a specified period or until you delete them. They are used to remember your preferences and provide a more personalized experience across multiple visits.

We regularly review our cookie retention periods to ensure they are appropriate for their intended purpose and comply with applicable regulations. We do not retain cookies longer than necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

Our use of cookies is based on several legal grounds, depending on the type of cookie and its purpose:

Consent: For certain types of cookies, particularly those used for analytics or by third-party services, we obtain your consent before setting these cookies. You can withdraw your consent at any time through your browser settings or by contacting us.
